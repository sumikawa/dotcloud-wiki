*COLOR [#t961f7a1]

Define the color of the header line in list display table.

- ''cur'' : ordered current line.
- ''etc'' : non-ordered line.

|Type|Color|h
|cur|BGCOLOR(#88ff88):|
|etc|BGCOLOR(#cccccc):|
